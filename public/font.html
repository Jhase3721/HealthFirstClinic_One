About HealthFirstClinic

HealthFirstClinic is led by Dr. Sheila May T. Buniel, a highly experienced general physician and Obstetrics and Gynecology (OB-GYN) specialist. With over 25 years of dedicated service in Tandag City, Dr. Buniel provides comprehensive healthcare, addressing a wide range of medical needs for patients of all ages—from routine check-ups and preventive care as a general physician to specialized OB-GYN services for women’s health. Her dual expertise ensures holistic care, making HealthFirstClinic a trusted choice for families. We are committed to your well-being.
